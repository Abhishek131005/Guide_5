Step 1: Read CSV Operator (Import Configuration Wizard- choose dataset)

Step 2: Set Role Operator [Edit Parameter List: attribute name(target); target role (label)]
*Join "out" pin of Read CSV to "exa" pin of Set Role*

Step 3: Split Data Operator [Edit Parameter List: ratio 0.7;0.3]
*Join "exa" pin of Set Role with "exa" pin of Split Data.*

Step 4: Naive Bayes Operator
*Join the "par" port of Split Data to the "tra" port of Naive Bayes*
 
Step 5: Apply Model Operator
*Join the "mod" pin of Naive Bayes to "mod" port of Apply Model*
*Join the "par" port of Split Data to the "unl" port of Apply Model*

Step 6: Performance Operator
*Join the "lab" port of Apply Model to the "lab" port of Performance Operator*
*Join the "mod" port of Apply Model to "res" port
*Join the "per" port of Performance to "res" port

Click "Run" 
Voila!
